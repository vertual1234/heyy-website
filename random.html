Life at Scarisbrick Hall School

What is Scarisbrick Hall School?

Scarisbrick Hall School is a co-educational independent day school in Lancashire, set within the historic Grade I listed Scarisbrick Hall, a Gothic Revival mansion designed by A.W.N. Pugin. It educates pupils from birth to 18 across Nursery, Pre-School, First, Middle, College, and Sixth Form, combining academic excellence with a strong focus on individual care and holistic development. Renowned for its top-tier Sixth Form, outstanding ISI inspection ratings, and 100% A-level pass rates, the school also enriches learning through initiatives like the Global Classroom and extensive outdoor education within its 50-acre grounds, fostering confident, well-rounded learners in a unique and inspiring setting.
